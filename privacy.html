Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

Livo collects the following information: • Account details: nickname, age, gender, and profile photo • Geographic location: to find nearby users, only while the app is active • Usage data: general app activity to improve the service

2. Use of Data

Your data is used solely to: • Show you users with similar intentions nearby • Enable connection and chat between synced users • Improve the service experience and secure your account

3. Data Sharing

Livo does not sell or transfer personal data to third parties, except: • Technical infrastructure providers required to operate the service (e.g. Supabase and Google Auth) • When required by law or court order

4. Storage & Security

Data is stored on encrypted, secure servers. Deleting your account will permanently remove all personal data within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: • Access the data we store about you • Correct or delete your data • Revoke location permission at any time via device settings • Fully delete your account from within the app settings
